Videos

This tab contains all video materials you are reviewing. You can watch each video, observe details, and note issues. The goal is to compare different versions, detect errors, and highlight improvements in editing, animation, and audio.

Analysis

This section breaks down what you observe in each video. You check for motion accuracy, visual continuity, and audio synchronization. You document errors, inconsistencies, or areas needing improvement. This helps maintain consistent quality across videos.

Analysis also identifies patterns in mistakes, tracks improvements, and ensures alignment with project standards. Notes here form the foundation for objective scoring.

Scoring

Scoring converts observations into measurable metrics. Each video is rated on realism, accuracy, audio-visual sync, and overall execution. Scores help prioritize corrections, compare versions, and maintain objectivity. They also provide performance feedback for the team.

Guidelines

This tab lists rules and standards for evaluation. It defines what constitutes acceptable quality, errors, and variations. Following guidelines ensures all evaluators are consistent and scoring remains uniform.

Guidelines also serve as a reference to avoid subjectivity, ensuring every evaluation aligns with project goals and quality expectations.

Result

This section summarizes evaluation outcomes. It includes total scores, feedback, and pass/fail indicators. Results are exportable for reporting and decision-making. This tab consolidates videos, analysis, scoring, and guidelines into a final actionable outcome.
