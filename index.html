The analysis tab provides a detailed breakdown of each evaluation metric. Each video was assessed for clarity, motion synchronization, audio quality, and realism. The scoring is based on a rubric that ensures consistency across all evaluations.

Observations from the analysis highlight strengths and weaknesses. Video 1 demonstrates superior motion realism, whereas Video 2 shows minor inconsistencies. These insights guide improvements in future video production.

Each video is assigned a score based on predefined criteria. Scores reflect motion, audio synchronization, visual clarity, and overall quality. Detailed scoring allows quick identification of areas needing improvement.

Annotation and evaluation follow strict guidelines. Videos are reviewed frame by frame to ensure accuracy. Notes are logged for motion, audio, and object detection accuracy.

Results summarize the evaluation and provide actionable recommendations. Video 1 is identified as the preferred reference due to its higher quality and natural motion.
