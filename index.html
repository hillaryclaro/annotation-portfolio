Evaluation Guidelines

  • Black screen, audio issues, generation differences must be QN’d.
  • Audio: hiss, hum, clicks, ambient noise.
  • Visual: grain, speckles, thermal noise, read noise, amplification noise.
  • Penalize model for deformities, distorted faces, unnatural limb movements.
